FAQ

Before your
first visit

How do I book?

Choose a service, preferred date and time, then send the prepared request through WhatsApp. This portfolio demo does not transmit your data to a server.

Can I change or cancel?

In a live studio version, you would reply in the same WhatsApp chat. A cancellation policy should be confirmed by the business before launch.

Which language can I use?

The interface is available in English, Russian, Turkish and German. The studio can later specify which languages are available for each appointment.

Are the prices final?

Prices on this concept site are examples. Hair length, material or treatment complexity may change a final quote.
